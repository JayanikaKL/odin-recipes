Waffles

This is the fluffiest pancakes you'll ever taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of melted butter
  • 1 Tbsp Baking powder
  • 1/4 Cup Sugar
  • 3/4 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Cups milk
  • 2 Tsps Vanilla extract

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a small bowl.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk the milk, eggs and vanilla together, Stir in the melted butter
  3. Add dry and wet mixtures untill combined
  4. Add about 1/3 cup of batter for each waffle
  5. When done, place on a wire rack until ready to serve so they stay crisp.
