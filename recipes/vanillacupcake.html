Vanilla cupcake

Everyone loves cupcakes! This is the only Vanilla Cupcake recipe you'll ever need.It's simple to make, and mouthwatering.

Ingredients

  • 125g Butter
  • 125g Castor sugar
  • 125g Self raising flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
    • 21/2 tbsp of milk
  • 1/4 tsp of bicarbonate of soda

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 170c
  2. Add all the Ingredients to a mixing bowl
  3. Beat on medium speed for 2 mins and then on high speed for another 5 mins
  4. Line a cupcake tray with cupcake liners
  5. Add the batter into the cupcake liners
  6. Bake at 170C for 22 mins
